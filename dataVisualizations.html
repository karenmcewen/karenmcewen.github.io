About my logo...

I created this logo using Tableau, as a visualization of a part of my own story.
The data source is the National Center for Education Statistics (NCES.gov)
Master's Degrees awarded in Engineering from 1950-2012.
Each point represents the percentage of those degrees which were awarded to women each year.
The increase is shown through circle size and color.
Blue (1-5%) * purple (6-10%) * salmon (my cohort - approximately 15%) * orange (15-20%) * red (20-23%).
