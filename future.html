The Future!

Did you know that with every breath
you are just a moment away from the future?

A few things I'm thinking about...

How do self driving cars know when the light is red? Is it visual, or an electronic signal?

We live on a constantly evolving planet - earthquakes, wildfires, tornados, tsunamis and other natural disasters happen every day. Only a tiny percentage of them make the news - who decides which to report on, and why?

There are black holes colliding in space, creating actual ripples in time that we can now measure! How do we even talk about an event that happened billions of years ago that we are only now observing? Is there a tense for that?
